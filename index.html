About Me

I'm Norman Junior Mohale, a passionate software developer with a flair for creating innovative solutions. Born and raised in the vibrant city of Boksburg, South Africa, I speak Sepedi, English, and Xitsonga. With a strong foundation from Falcon Education School and a Diploma in Software Development from Rosebank College, my skills span C#, Java, HTML5, SQL, and Microsoft Office. Beyond code, I thrive on storytelling and creativity, often found diving into fantasy novels or penning my own tales. My journey is driven by curiosity and a love for problem-solving, and I invite you to explore my work and join me on this exciting adventure. Let's create something extraordinary together!
